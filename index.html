Game Rules

  • The goal of the game is to get a higher total score than the dealer without going over 21
  • After starting, you must first select a wager; you start with $1000
  • Both you and the dealer are given 2 cards to start, but you can only see one of the dealer's cards
  • You can either Hit (receive another card) or Stand (pass turn to the dealer)
  • On the Dealer's turn, they will continue to hit until they have a higher/equal score, or bust
  • Aces can be worth either 1 or 11 points
  • Face cards are worth 10 points
  • The deck of cards is shuffled after every round
  • When you run out of cash it's game over
